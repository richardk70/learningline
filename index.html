Services

Services include individualized speech and language therapy, evaluations, and consultations. Services are provided in office or in the client's natural learning environment, such as in home, daycare, or school site. All services are by appointment only. Call today for a free, initial consultation!
What is Dyslexia?
Dyslexia is a learning difference characterized by difficulty learning to read and spell. It is not indicative of intellectual deficits.
 
What is Phonemic Awareness?
Phonemic Awareness is the ability to hear, identify, and manipulate individual sounds in spoken words. Dyslexic students who lack phonemic awareness cannot learn how to read and spell.
Consultations
Consultations gather general information about a client, including primary concerns, previous therapy, assessments, and present diagnoses. Consultations serve as a chance to meet the client, family, and/or related professionals to discuss the best approaches towards meeting the client's communication needs and goals. When a child underachieves in school, it is crucial to identify the underlying cause as soon as possible. As a rule, the older a child is, the harder it is to address a learning difference. We offer consultations to evaluate your child’s screening and assessment needs. This initial consultation can provide:
  • Preliminary evaluation regarding likelihood of presence of dyslexia or phonemic awareness deficit.
  • Recommendations regarding further assessments, and if required, referrals to trusted professionals.
  • Information about appropriate schooling and tutoring options in the Los Angeles area.
  • How Learning Line can or cannot meet your student’s needs.
  • Correct steps to take to get your child on a successful learning path.
  • General information about Dyslexia.
  • Information on advocating for, and receiving testing and accommodation at public schools.

Evaluations
The evaluation assesses all areas of communication, including receptive and expressive language, articulation, phonology, oral motor, voice and fluency, and social pragmatic skills. The evaluation includes formal standardized testing, informal observation, and parent and/or client interview. Treatment plan and recommendations are provided based on outcome.
Tutoring
We offer after school tutoring. These sessions are appropriate for students having either a deficit in Phonemic Awareness and/or Dyslexia. These learning differences commonly occur together. All can be corrected with regular tutoring using research-based, simultaneous, multi-sensory methods. We feature the Milan Method and the Barton Reading and Spelling System.

I’ve Tried Tutoring, But It Didn’t Work
These learning differences require specialized, highly-targeted techniques. Most tutoring centers do not have the trained staff to successfully remediate these learning differences and, in fact, can often do more harm than good as a result.

How Do I Know If My Child Has These Learning Differences?
Dyslexia and a lack of phonemic awareness can be accurately identified as early as kindergarten. Before a student is accepted into the tutoring program, they must have specific academic testing by a trained professional. See Screenings and Consultations for more information.

Policies

Insurance
Learning Line.org is considered an out-of-network provider. All services are self-pay. We do not file with any insurance companies. However, we will gladly provide a monthly invoice that can be submitted to your insurance company for an out-of-network reimbursement if applicable. Appropriate CPT procedure and diagnostic codes will clearly be indicated on the invoice.

While a doctor's referral is often necessary for getting authorization and coverage from your insurance company, your child can be seen without a referral. Please check with your insurance company for more information regarding reimbursement and whether pre-authorization is necessary.

Fees

Please contact our office directly for more information regarding individual fees. The fee for therapy is based on an hourly rate and includes time spent in direct therapy with the child and consultation with the family. An individual therapy session begins at $90 for a 60-minute session. Shorter sessions may be discussed, particularly for children under the age of 3.

Cancellations
A 24-hour notice is required in the event of a cancellation. Missed appointments without a 24-hour notice are subject to a cancellation fee. We understand that unforeseen circumstances and sicknesses do happen. In the event of a sickness, please call to give notice of your cancellation as soon as possible.

The success of your therapy sessions depends on consistency. The child's plan and goals are based on having a consistent treatment schedule. In the event of a cancellation, a re-scheduled appointment is strongly encouraged to maintain progress.
