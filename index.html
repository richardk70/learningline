Services

Services Offered
Services include individualized speech and language therapy, evaluations, and consultations. Services are provided in office or in the client's natural learning environment, such as in home, daycare, or school site. All services are by appointment only. Call today for a free, initial consultation!
Consultations
Consultations gather general information about a client, including primary concerns, previous therapy, assessments, and present diagnoses. Consultations serve as a chance to meet the client, family, and/or related professionals to discuss the best approaches towards meeting the client's communication needs and goals.
Evaluations
The evaluation assesses all areas of communication, including receptive and expressive language, articulation, phonology, oral motor, voice and fluency, and social pragmatic skills. The evaluation includes formal standardized testing, informal observation, and parent and/or client interview. Treatment plan and recommendations are provided based on outcome.
Therapy
Speech and language treatment programs are developed based on goals and current individual needs. Bernadette utilizes a variety of techniques and encourages team collaboration with parents, caregivers, and other related professionals to maximize communication success. Therapy services are available for the following areas of concern:
  • Early Language Delays ("Late Talkers")
  • Expressive and Receptive Language Disorders
  • Articulation/Phonological Disorders
  • Autism Spectrum Disorders/Social Pragmatic Skills
  • Cognitive Language
  • Auditory Processing
  • Verbal Apraxia and Oral-Motor Disorders
  • Fluency Disorders/Stuttering
  • Feeding and Orofacial Myology Disorders (Tongue Thrust)
  • Literacy and Phonemic Awareness
  • Reading Comprehension

Policies

Insurance
Learning Line.org is considered an out-of-network provider. All services are self-pay. We do not file with any insurance companies. However, we will gladly provide a monthly invoice that can be submitted to your insurance company for an out-of-network reimbursement if applicable. Appropriate CPT procedure and diagnostic codes will clearly be indicated on the invoice.

While a doctor's referral is often necessary for getting authorization and coverage from your insurance company, your child can be seen without a referral. Please check with your insurance company for more information regarding reimbursement and whether pre-authorization is necessary.

Fees

Please contact our office directly for more information regarding individual fees. The fee for therapy is based on an hourly rate and includes time spent in direct therapy with the child and consultation with the family. An individual therapy session begins at $90 for a 60-minute session. Shorter sessions may be discussed, particularly for children under the age of 3.

Cancellations
A 24-hour notice is required in the event of a cancellation. Missed appointments without a 24-hour notice are subject to a cancellation fee. We understand that unforeseen circumstances and sicknesses do happen. In the event of a sickness, please call to give notice of your cancellation as soon as possible.

The success of your therapy sessions depends on consistency. The child's plan and goals are based on having a consistent treatment schedule. In the event of a cancellation, a re-scheduled appointment is strongly encouraged to maintain progress.
